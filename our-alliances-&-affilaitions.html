Our Alliances & Affiliations

A-Star Academy is proud to be affiliated with City & Guilds, a globally recognized leader in skills development, offering internationally accredited diploma programs. This affiliation ensures that our students receive industry-relevant training, enhancing their career prospects in India and abroad.

Additionally, A-Star Academy is affiliated with the Tourism & Hospitality Skill Council (THSC), a government-recognized body under the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC). This affiliation strengthens our commitment to industry-aligned education, equipping students with the expertise and qualifications required to excel in the dynamic hospitality sector.
