CAMPUS LIFE SECTION

The A-Star Advantage

What makes us the black sheep of hospitality education?

Game-Changing Pedagogy

Unlike other hospitality colleges, A-Star Academy throws you into the heart of the action from day one. Gain hands-on experience in a real 5-star hotel environment, learning both on the job and in the classroom simultaneously.

Earn While You Learn

We believe in empowering our students with entrepreneurial skills. You'll have the opportunity to lead and manage an operational fine-dining restaurant, driving revenue while gaining invaluable experience. The cherry on top? Earn a commission on the revenue generated!

Strategic Industry Connections

Through our partnership with the prestigious Hotel Sahara Star (a leading 5-star deluxe hotel in Mumbai), internships and job placements are practically guaranteed for A-Star Academy graduates. Our connections extend beyond India, with affiliations with top international colleges and exciting student exchange programs.
