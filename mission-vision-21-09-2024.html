Mission, Vision and Values

To empower aspiring hospitality professionals with the knowledge, skills, and experience they need to excel in the dynamic industry. We strive to create a nurturing environment where students can learn from industry experts, gain practical experience, and develop a passion for hospitality.

ABOUT A-STAR ACADEMY :

With campuses in the vibrant city of Mumbai and the serene Aamby Valley City, A-Star Academy is more than just a school; it's a springboard for aspiring leaders to make their mark in the global sphere of hospitality. We offer a comprehensive 3-year hotel management program that equips you with the skills and knowledge to excel in all aspects of hotel operations.

Our team of renowned chefs and industry veterans provides in-depth instruction, from culinary arts and food service management to hospitality operations and guest relations. A-Star Academy offers a transformative learning experience that prepares you for a fulfilling career in the world of hospitality.
