Sahara Star, a flagship 5-star hotel of Sahara India Pariwar, is located near Mumbai's domestic and international airports. It combines Indian mysticism and culture with modern technology and hospitality.


The hotel features the world’s largest pillar-less clear-to-sky dome, an architectural marvel that blends hospitality and entertainment.


The grand lobby, complemented by a 4,000 sq ft Marine Aquarium with a Private Dining Room, offers a unique dining experience. It also boasts the world's first 'hemisphere' glass panoramic elevators rising in a 55,000 sq ft tropical lagoon.


Sahara Sapphire, Mumbai’s largest multi-purpose event hall, spans 55,000 sq ft with a 25ft ceiling height. The Jade Ballroom, with its contemporary design, can host up to 1,000 guests in a 10,000 sq ft pillar-less space.


The hotel offers 354 luxurious guest rooms, including suites set amidst a tropical lagoon, providing stunning city views and exquisite dining options.


For relaxation, the Spa and Wellness Centre spans 19,000 sq ft, featuring unique therapies, a gym, and an outdoor pool with a jacuzzi. Additionally, a preview theatre with luxurious seating allows guests to enjoy iconic movie screenings.


Sahara Star seamlessly blends Indian culture and hospitality with modern amenities, making it a premier destination for both business and leisure.
