A-Star Academy: Sahara-Star Campus

Imagine yourself: Stepping into the heart of Mumbai, a vibrant city buzzing with energy. A-Star Academy's Sahara Star Campus, nestled within this iconic 5-star hotel, offers a unique opportunity to learn from industry experts in a luxurious setting.

You're learning the art of hospitality from seasoned professionals. You're getting hands-on experience in world-class kitchens, learning to curate menus and deliver exceptional service. The iconic pillar-less dome becomes your classroom, inspiring creativity and innovation.

Take a break and explore the hotel's many offerings. The Sahara Star provides a stimulating environment that goes beyond academics.

The A-Star Advantage:
  • Gain practical experience in a renowned 5-star hotel.
  • Learn from industry leaders in hospitality management.
  • Network with professionals and build a strong foundation for your career.

A-Star Academy's Sahara Star Campus offers an unparalleled learning experience.
