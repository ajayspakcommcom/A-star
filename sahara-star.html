Welcome to A- Star Academy at Hotel Sahara Star, Mumbai

Nestled within the iconic Hotel Sahara Star, A- Star Academy offers a world-class learning environment in the heart of Mumbai, India's entertainment capital. Our Mumbai campus is more than just an academic institution—it is an inspiring space where ambition meets excellence, providing students with access to premium resources, real-world exposure, and an atmosphere that fosters creativity and growth.


A Learning Hub in a Prestigious Setting

Housed within one of Mumbai’s most celebrated luxury hotels, A- Star Academy benefits from the sophisticated infrastructure and dynamic energy of Sahara Star. Students are immersed in an ecosystem that seamlessly blends hospitality, technology, and innovation—offering a unique perspective into the world of business, events, and entertainment.

With its world-class amenities, luxurious event spaces, and vibrant atmosphere, Sahara Star serves as the perfect backdrop for students to interact with industry professionals, gain hands-on experience, and refine their skills in an inspiring environment.

About Hotel Sahara Star

A flagship 5-star hotel of Sahara India Pariwar, Sahara Star is a landmark of luxury and architectural brilliance. The hotel features the world’s largest pillar-less clear-to-sky dome, creating an immersive space that blends Indian mysticism with modern elegance.

The grand lobby, complemented by a 4,000 sq. ft. Marine Aquarium and a Private Dining Room, offers a one-of-a-kind experience, while the hotel’s hemisphere glass panoramic elevators rise within a 55,000 sq.ft. tropical lagoon, setting an extraordinary scene of opulence and serenity.


Unparalleled Spaces & Luxury Amenities

For relaxation, the Spa and Wellness Centre spans 19,000 sq. ft., featuring unique therapies, a gym, and an outdoor pool with a jacuzzi. Additionally, a preview theatre with luxurious seating allows guests to enjoy iconic movie screenings.

• Sahara Sapphire is Mumbai’s largest multi-purpose event hall, spanning 55,000 sq. ft. with a 25-ft ceiling height.

• Jade Ballroom is a spacious 10,000 sq. ft. pillar-less venue that can accommodate up to 1,000 guests.

• Experience luxury with 354 elegantly designed guest rooms, offering breathtaking panoramic city views.

• Indulge in relaxation at the 19,000 sq. ft. Spa & Wellness Centre, featuring a modern gym, jacuzzi, and exclusive therapies.

• Enjoy a premium cinematic experience in the private preview theatre, designed with luxurious seating.

At A- Star Academy, students don’t just learn—they thrive in an environment surrounded by luxury, industry leaders, and state-of-the-art infrastructure, gaining an education that prepares them for the future while embracing the vibrant spirit of Sahara Star.
