about us

To The Journey Ahead

In a first of its kind concept in India, A-Star Academy is the only Hotel school which provides its students with a daily exposure to live functioning of a 5 Star Hotel. Here students live in real hotel environment and get a taste of the inner workings of the hotel operations from day one!

Why Choose A-Star

HOLISTIC GROWTH & REAL-WORLD EXPOSURE

Every student gets exposed to different hands – on functioning of hotel environment both as an observer as well as gaining practical experience in QSR, ODC Catering, Institutional Catering among others.

LEADERSHIP & MANAGEMENT FOCUS

Sharp emphasis on developing leadership and management skills. Opportunities to lead teams and work in management roles while studying.

STRATEGIC PARTNERSHIPS

Through partnership with our group owned Hotel Sahara star, a leading 5-star deluxe hotel in Mumbai with multiple restaurants and banqueting facilities, we facilitate internships and job placements for our students. Affiliations with the best of colleges abroad & exchange program.

DIVERSE EXTRACURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

Beyond the classroom experiences (Golfing, horse riding, swimming, and adventure sports). Cultural enrichment through fine arts such as painting, music, and dance. For the overall grooming and honing of the softer skills for an all rounded personality.

Earn While You Learn

Entrepreneurship training and real opportunities to apply it. Students can run the A-Star Restaurant, maximizing profits in a real-world business.
