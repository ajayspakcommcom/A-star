Who we are

"Welcome to A-Star Academy, India's only hotel school where you become a hotelier on day one."

Our Approach :

At A-Star Academy, we offer a ground-breaking educational experience unlike any other. Our dedication to experiential learning sets us apart, allowing our students to live and breathe hospitality from the very beginning.

Unique Curriculum :

Our curriculum is designed to provide practical exposure to the inner workings of a 5-Star property on a daily basis. From live kitchen experiences to hands-on training in various hospitality divisions, our programs go beyond traditional academic learning.

Campus Locations :

We have two distinct campuses tailored to meet the unique needs of our students. Our residential campus in Aamby Valley City offers a tranquil retreat amidst nature, while our Mumbai campus at Hotel Sahara Star provides accessibility for day scholars.

Wide Range of Courses :

We offer a wide spectrum of international and Indian culinary programs and hospitality courses catering to professionals, students, homemakers and hobby enthusiasts alike. Whether you're seeking a professional degree, diploma or short-term course, we have something for everyone.

Entrepreneurship Opportunities:

We empower our students with entrepreneurship training and real opportunities to apply their skills in a practical setting. Initiatives like the A-Star Restaurant allow students to run a real-world business, gaining invaluable entrepreneurial experience.

Experienced Faculty :

Led by experienced industry professionals and former chefs, our faculty is dedicated to providing world-class education grounded in real-world experience. Our mentors are passionate about teaching and preparing students for the challenges and opportunities of the hospitality sector.

Our Vision :

At A-Star Academy, we envision moulding future hospitality leaders, equipping them to shine in an ever-changing industry. Join us and embark on a journey to become A-Star in hospitality.
