Beyond the Classroom

Experience learning that extends beyond traditional settings. Engage in practical, real-world scenarios to apply theoretical knowledge in tangible ways.

Real Hotel Experience & Entrepreneurship

Work within a fully operational hotel environment, gaining firsthand experience in entrepreneurship. Run ventures like A-Star Restaurant/Cafeteria, learning to maximize profits through real-world business operations.

Holistic Growth at A-Star Campus

Aamby Valley City's campus promotes overall development, offering activities like golf, horse riding, swimming, and adventure sports, fostering a well-rounded educational experience.

Cultural Enrichment

Dive into the world of fine arts with opportunities in painting, music, and dance, enriching your cultural perspective and artistic skills.

Vineyard Visits: Grape to Glass

Explore the nearby Sula Vineyards in Nasik, a haven for wine enthusiasts, to learn about the wine-making process from grape cultivation to the finished product.

Rich Indian Artifacts

Discover India's heritage through a diverse collection of artifacts from across the country, enhancing your understanding of its rich cultural tapestry.

Specialized Library Resources

Access a comprehensive library dedicated to the hospitality and culinary arts, providing a wealth of knowledge to support your academic and professional journey.

Practical Catering Exposure

Gain practical exposure in various sectors of the hospitality industry, including hotels, quick- service restaurants, outdoor catering, and institutional catering, to broaden your professional experience.
