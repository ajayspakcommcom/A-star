Professional Diploma in Food Production & Patisserie

Brief: The Professional Diploma in Food Production and Patisserie is a specialized program crafted for individuals passionate about the culinary arts and patisserie, equipping them with comprehensive skills to excel in the hospitality industry.

This program offers a perfect blend of practical and theoretical knowledge of various cuisines along with Patisserie techniques, creative exploration, to create well-rounded culinary professionals.

Duration: 9 Months. (The first 6 months of the program comprises of periodic tutorials and practical classes followed by 3 months of paid internship)
Age: 17.5 yrs.
Eligibility: 12th Pass
Professional Diploma in Food Production & Patisserie
Sr no. Module
1 Introduction to Culinary & Pastry Arts
2 Fundamental Culinary Skills & Pastry Techniques
3 Soups, Sauces, Cuts of Vegetable & Meat
4 Knife Skills & Cooking Methods
5 Bread Making, Cakes and Sponges
6 Frosting, Chocolate Making, Pastries
7 Plating Styles
8 Food Safety and Kitchen Management
9 Contemporary Culinary Trends
10 Food Science & Nutrition
