Why Choose A star Academy ?

The A-Star
Advantage

What makes A-Star Academy unique? Our innovative pedagogy blends theory with real-world learning. With 100% internship placements, global career opportunities, and world-class facilities, we provide the perfect launchpad for hospitality professionals, equipping them with hands-on skills to thrive in this dynamic industry.

Game-Changing Pedagogy

A-Star Academy takes you straight into the action, offering a 5-star hotel learning environment from day one. Our students gain hands-on experience while mastering hospitality through both classroom learning and real-world exposure, making them industry-ready with practical training in every aspect of the field.

Career options in Hospitality

At A-Star Academy, students develop entrepreneurial skills while managing fine-dining operations. This unique hands-on experience helps them understand revenue management, gain real-world expertise, and even earn a commission on revenue—all while preparing for a successful career in the global hospitality industry.

Our Alliances & Affiliations

Through our partnership with Hotel Sahara Star, A-Star Academy guarantees top-tier internships and job placements. Our global affiliations extend beyond India, providing students with international learning opportunities and student exchange Programmes, opening doors to promising careers in hospitality worldwide.
