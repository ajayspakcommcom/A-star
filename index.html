Why Choose A-star Academy ?

The A-Star Advantage

Blending theory with real-world learning, A-Star offers 100% internships, global career pathways & world-class facilities. The perfect launchpad for tomorrow’s hospitality leaders.

Game-Changing Pedagogy

Students train in a 5-star hotel environment, mastering skills through classroom learning and real-world exposure—industry readiness in action.

Career options in Hospitality

A-Star opens doors to careers across the global hospitality industry—from hotels and resorts to fine dining, equipping students with real-world expertise & professional confidence.

Our Alliances & Affiliations

In collaboration with Savitribai Phule Pune University, and backed by Aamby Valley City & Hotel Sahara Star, A-Star delivers prestigious degrees with unmatched real-world exposure.
