Welcome to A Star Academy at Aamby Valley City, Lonavala

Set amidst the breathtaking landscapes of the Sahyadri mountains, A Star Academy’s Lonavala campus is an immersive educational retreat within the prestigious Aamby Valley City —India’s first planned hill city. Spanning over 10,000 acres of pristine beauty, this campus is designed to inspire creativity, ambition, and excellence.

Here, learning transcends the classroom, as students are surrounded by lush greenscapes, serene waterfronts, and world-class infrastructure that foster innovation and holistic development. The A Star Academy experience at Aamby Valley City oEers an unparalleled opportunity to engage with industry experts, develop real-world skills, and gain insights in an environment that seamlessly blends education, luxury, and nature.


A Learning Experience Like No Other

Within Aamby Valley City , students find themselves in an ecosystem that redefines premium living. This gated and secured destination offers the perfect blend of modern amenities and natural tranquility, allowing students to learn, create, and evolve in a setting that encourages both academic excellence and personal growth.

The city itself is a testament to human ingenuity , with meticulously planned infrastructure, iconic venues, and recreational spaces that inspire a progressive and enriched learning experience. Whether through industry collaborations, workshops, or interactive sessions, A Star Academy at Aamby Valley City ensures that students receive an education that prepares them for the future while embracing the art of refined living.

About Aamby Valley City

A fusion of nature and human innovation, Aamby Valley City is more than just a destination—it’s a lifestyle statement. With its vast open spaces, awe-inspiring greenscapes, and luxurious settings, the city oEers every modern convenience in a tranquil, scenic environment.

• World-class infrastructure, blending nature with modern design.

• Expansive landscapes, where the horizon extends as far as the eye can see.

• Exclusive privileges, offering unmatched luxury and lifestyle experiences.

• A dynamic canvas of nature, where every season brings a new perspective to life.

At A Star Academy, Lonavala, students don’t just learn—they thrive in an extraordinary world , surrounded by excellence, nature, and limitless possibilities .
