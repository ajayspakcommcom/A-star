This fully residential academy is situated in the picturesque Aamby Valley City - independent India’s first planned hill city nestled amidst the mighty Sahyadris, and spans over 10,000 acres. It is located at a distance of 120 km from Mumbai, 90 km from Pune and lies 25 km off the town of Lonavala on the Mumbai Pune Expressway. The architecture of our campus is designed to provide students with a feel of modernity and cultural heritage with unique murals and Indian artifacts from across India.

Our school offers the widest spectrum of International as well as Indian culinary and hospitality courses, catering to professionals, students, homemakers and hobby

enthusiasts. From professional degree and diploma programs to short-term single courses, we provide tailored learning opportunities to meet the diverse needs and interests of our students and entrepreneurs alike.

Our faculty comprises of experienced industry professionals and former chefs with a wealth of knowledge and expertise in the culinary and hospitality sectors. Their practical insights, coupled with a passion for teaching, ensure that students receive world-class education and mentorship that is grounded in real-world experience.

HOLISTIC GROWTH & REAL-WORLD EXPOSURE

Embrace hands-on learning from day one in India's first hotel school that embeds you in the heart of a live 5-star hotel environment, fostering practical skills and real-world insights every day.

A-Star'S PRINCIPLE

Our core principle is the seamless integration of theory with practical experience, ensuring that education transcends traditional classrooms to include immersive, real-time exposure to hospitality operations.

KEY OF SUCCESS

Our success is built on a unique educational model that combines expert-led teaching, state-of-the-art facilities, and direct industry engagement, preparing students for immediate employability in the global hospitality sector.
